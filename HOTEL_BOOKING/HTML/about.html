Our Story

Established in 2008, CSK Hotels emerged as a beacon of luxury and comfort, bringing the vibrant culture and unparalleled hospitality of Chennai to the world. Just as the Chennai Super Kings have built a legacy of success in the Indian Premier League, we’ve crafted an enduring reputation in the hospitality industry for exceptional service, elegance, and an unbeatable guest experience.
